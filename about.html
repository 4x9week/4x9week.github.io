How do I download the wallpapers?

Click on the thumbnail of the wallpaper style you want. This will open a notion page where you can download all 9 colors for the chosen style.

Hover over the image and click the arrow icon to download. Or click the "..." icon, then click download.

How do you make the wallpapers?

We take a screenshot of the panel from an manga page or panel image already existing on the web, then manipulate the color to match one of our 9 colors.

We take extreme care to only use images directly from the author so that the correct credits can be applied and we are not sharing other people‚Äôs art. Those credits can be found at the top of the download page for each wallpaper style, as well as in the descriptions of any 4x9week social media post about the wallpapers (currently only Instagram and Pinterest are active).

Is this legal?

Technically yes. We do not aim to harm the integrity of the work or the creators by sharing the wallpapers. Our goal is to celebrate the media that we enjoy by providing a little piece of it that you can use as a wallpaper. Completely free for you. You can think of this being similar to sharing a gif or meme of the anime you like.
